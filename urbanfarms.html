Samruddhi Urban Farms

The Property

Samruddhi is spread across 80+ acres of land. It is a place of pristine beauty, serene atmosphere, and tranquil surroundings. At the centre of the property is a modern and well-equipped clubhouse with all the amenities.

The Investment

Around 200+ premium farm land owners have already invested in this property. Of which few of the investors have settled down there; while some have rented out their villas as homestays.For those who plan it as investment, you can also explore the option of building bamboo houses and renting it out for Samruddhi Club World visitors.

At Present

At present, there are 50+ villa plots for SALE! Owning a piece of land in this idyllic location is truly a wise investment. It is not only a stable asset but also a wonderful option for those planning to settle down in a place close to nature. Though located at the heart of the city, it seems to be miles away from the humdrum of city life.
